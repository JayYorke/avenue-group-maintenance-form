Property Maintenance Checklist

Estimated Time: 10-15 minutes | Tools Needed: None (visual/basic operational checks only)
Interior Checks (~8-10 minutes)
9. Primary Lights & Switches

Test main room lighting to ensure electrical function

10. Entry Doors & Main Windows

Verify doors latch/lock securely and windows close properly

11. Walls & Ceilings

Scan for new cracks, water stains, or visible mold growth

12. High-Traffic Flooring

Check main walkways for damage, loose tiles, or trip hazards

13. Electrical Outlets

Visual inspection of one outlet per room for damage or scorch marks

Exterior Checks (~5 minutes)
14. Entry Walkways & Steps

Inspect for cracks, loose railings, or safety hazards

15. Roof & Siding (Ground View)

Look for missing shingles, siding damage, or obvious issues

16. Gutters & Downspouts

Check for visible blockages, detachment, or overflow damage

17. HVAC Unit & Vents

Ensure outdoor unit is clear of debris and vents are unobstructed

18. Exterior Faucets

Visual check for leaks, freeze damage, or hose connection issues

19. Foundation & Pest Signs

Quick scan for obvious pest activity, nests, or foundation concerns

20. Garage Door (if applicable)

Test operation and safety sensor functionality
